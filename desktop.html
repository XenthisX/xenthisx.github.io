WATERCOOLING A DESKTOP

A journey in computer design.

MORE PICTURES MORE INFO

    TITAN is the name that I gave to my senior project. While building a computer is fairly straightforward, involving simple slotting of parts into a chassis, Titan was a much more complex project.

    Titan added difficulties of bending rigid PETG tubing and putting distilled water into a closed-loop pump system that I designed myself, as well as challenges in aesthetically pleasing computer design.

The senior project at Tandem Friends School is a self-driven capstone project designed to allow students to explore an area of interest to them.
